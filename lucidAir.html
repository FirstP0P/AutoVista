Exquisite Comfort
The Lucid Air sets a new standard in spacious luxury, prioritizing passenger comfort and practicality. With an expansive 108 cm (42.5 inches) of legroom for front passengers, drivers can enjoy extended journeys with ample space to stretch out. Adding to its appeal is the Lucid Air's impressive cargo capacity, featuring approximately 9.9 cubic feet (280 liters) of frunk space and an additional 11.1 cubic feet (314 liters) in the rear trunk. Whether for daily errands or extended road trips, the Lucid Air delivers on both luxury and functionality.
