  • Aerodynamics & Engine
    • The EVO design prioritizes aerodynamics, utilizing a front splitter to create a double airway beneath the body and new flaps that increase aerodynamic load five times compared to the previous Huracán model, enhancing stability during braking, acceleration, and cornering. A newly designed suspended rear spoiler further improves aerodynamic efficiency. The V10 aspirated engine, boasting the highest specific power, features optimized fluid dynamics in both aspiration and exhaust systems, drawing from Lamborghini Motorsport expertise to ensure responsive performance at any speed. The dual-clutch transmission (LDF) is enhanced for peak performance, complemented by a dynamic, vibrant exhaust sound.
  • 4-Wheel Drive
    • The Huracán EVO combines Lamborghini Dynamic Steering and Lamborghini All-Wheel Steering for unparalleled dynamic performance. The LDS system adjusts steering based on speed and driving mode, while the LRS system manages rear-steering for agility at low speeds and stability at higher speeds. Electronically controlled 4-wheel drive enhances torque distribution, optimizing control in all conditions. Continuous adaptation based on driving mode and road response improves power transfer during sporty driving and enhances traction and safety in everyday situations. Overall, the Huracán EVO offers exceptional control and performance across various driving scenarios.

Journey Of Huracán

  • 2014 - Introduction of the Huracan Series The year 2014 marked the debut of the Lamborghini Huracan series, succeeding the popular Gallardo. The Huracan LP 610-4 was unveiled, featuring a 5.2-liter V10 engine producing 610 horsepower and an advanced all-wheel-drive system. It set a new standard for performance and design in the supercar segment, laying the foundation for future variants.
  • 2017 - Launch of the Huracan Performante In 2017, Lamborghini introduced the Huracan Performante, a track-focused variant that pushed the boundaries of performance even further. With enhanced aerodynamics, lightweight materials, and a more powerful engine, it delivered unparalleled track performance and set records at various race circuits worldwide. The Performante showcased Lamborghini's commitment to pushing the limits of speed and agility.
  • 2019 - Introduction of the Huracan Evo PThe year 2019 saw the official introduction of the Lamborghini Huracan Evo. Building upon the success of its predecessors, the Evo incorporated advanced technologies such as the Aerodinamica Lamborghini Attiva (ALA) system and a predictive logic system for dynamic vehicle control. These innovations further improved the car's performance, handling, and driver engagement, solidifying its position as a top contender in the supercar market.
  • Present Day As of the current day, the Lamborghini Huracan Evo continues to captivate automotive enthusiasts worldwide. With its iconic design, cutting-edge technology, and uncompromising performance, it remains a symbol of Lamborghini's commitment to innovation and excellence. Various special editions and new variants have been introduced to cater to different driving preferences, ensuring that the Huracan Evo remains competitive and desirable in the ever-evolving supercar landscape.
